For the one who means so much to me

I Am Sorry...

I know today is your birthday, and this day should only be filled with happiness, smiles, love and beautiful memories. But before wishing you with my whole heart, I want to say something that I have been carrying inside me.

I am sorry for last time. I know I did not wish you properly, and I know that it must have hurt you. The truth is, I did it purposely because of some reason, but that does not make it right. No reason can completely justify making someone special feel ignored on their birthday.

Last year was your first birthday in our relationship, and I know it should have been special. It should have been full of warmth, excitement, love and beautiful words. But instead, I made it incomplete. Maybe I did not express it then, but deep inside I knew that I had missed a moment that mattered.

I am not writing this to make the past perfect, because I cannot change what already happened. But I can honestly accept it. I can say sorry from my heart. I can promise that your special days, your feelings, and your smile matter to me more than my ego, my mood, or any situation.

You are special to me. Your birthday is special to me. And this time, I wanted to do something different, something made with effort, time and feelings. This website is not just made with code. It is made with all the things I could not say properly before.

So today, on your birthday, I want to wish you with my whole heart: may your life always be filled with happiness, success, peace, love and endless reasons to smile. May you always feel valued, cared for and celebrated, especially by me.

I am sorry for the birthday I made incomplete last time. And I hope this little surprise makes this birthday a little more special.

Happy Birthday, my special one. 🎂💖
